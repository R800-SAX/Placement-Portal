Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to typical questions about registration, recruiter profile approval, and student resumes.

Students can sign up by clicking the "Sign In / Register" button at the top-right, selecting the student registration link, filling out their academic stats (HSC, SSC, UG CGPA), and uploading a resume. The account is activated once approved by the administrator.
All company and recruiter accounts undergo verification by the placement coordinator administrators. Once approved, recruiters will receive an email confirmation and can proceed to post job openings.
Students can upload their resumes in PDF format (preferred), DOC, or DOCX. The maximum file size limit is 16MB. Ensure that the uploaded file matches details in your academic profile.
