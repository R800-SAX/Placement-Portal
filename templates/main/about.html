About PlaceTrack AI

Providing bridge connectivity between graduating student cohorts and leading technology employers.

Our Mission

The Training and Placement Department facilitates the process of placement of students passing out from the College besides collaborating with leading organizations and institutes in setting up internship and training programs of high value.

Recruitment Drives

The cell is well-equipped with infrastructure to support every stage of placement, including audio-visual lecture rooms, written test halls, computer setups for online coding examinations, group discussions chambers, and private interview booths.

Key Coordinators

Our training and placement program is driven by student cell coordinators and senior faculty members who ensure that drives run smoothly and candidate qualifications align with target companies.
